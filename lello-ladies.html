What is Lello Ladies?

A modern, gentle space for women.

Lello Ladies is for the woman who wants to trust herself again. Who's curious about intuition but doesn't want a smoke ceremony. Who wants a circle, not a cult.

Oracle cards, women's circles, retreats, journals and gentle tools for the work that happens quietly, inside.
