About the book

From the spotlight of Australian television to quiet rooms full of ghosts she's seen since she was a child — Chezzi tells the story she's been carrying her whole life.

It's a book about intuition, identity, motherhood, grief and the courage it takes to stop hiding the strangest, truest parts of yourself. Honest, often funny, occasionally unsettling — it's the kind of memoir you'll finish and immediately want to talk about.
